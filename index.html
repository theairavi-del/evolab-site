What's Lion's Mane?

Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus) contains hericenones and erinacines — compounds that stimulate Nerve Growth Factor (NGF) production. NGF is essential for the growth, maintenance, and survival of neurons.

Unlike typical brain supplements, Lion's Mane can cross the blood-brain barrier, delivering benefits directly to neural tissue. It's been used in traditional Chinese medicine for over 2,000 years.

Elevating Nature

Cognitive Benefits for Kids

Recent studies suggest Lion's Mane supports working memory, focus duration, and cognitive flexibility in children. The mushroom's neurotrophic compounds help build and maintain the neural connections essential for learning.

BrainRoar delivers 500mg per serving — the amount used in clinical studies showing measurable improvements in attention and memory tasks.

Importantly, Lion's Mane is completely safe for children. It's simply a delicious edible mushroom with no psychedelic properties.
